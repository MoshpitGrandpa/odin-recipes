Fried Eggs

Description

It's a fried egg, bro. What rock have you been living under.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • ??????
  • Profit

Steps

  1. Preheat cast iron pan over med-low heat
  2. When handle of pan becomes hot, add 1T of butter
  3. After butter foams and subsides, crack egg into pan
  4. Turn down heat to low. Let egg cook until whites are set
  5. If bottom is cooking too fast, place lid loosely on pan to cook whites faster
