Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is an American classic struggle-food.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Head pan, preferably cast iron
  2. While pan is heating, add cheese to slice of bread
  3. Place other slice of bread on top
  4. Butter outside of bread
  5. Place sandwich in pan, cook on medium heat until golden brown on one side, flip sandwich
  6. Repeat on other side
